Trevor Noah's Biography

Trevor Noah (born 20 February 1984) is a South African comedian, screenwriter, television producer, political commentator, actor, radio personality, author and television host. He is the host of The Daily Show, an American satirical news program on Comedy Central.. Born in Johannesburg, Noah began his career as a comedian, presenter, and actor in South Africa in 2002.

Noah has won various awards, including an MTV Africa Music Award and a Primetime Emmy Award from eleven nominations. He was named one of "The 35 Most Powerful People in New York Media" by The Hollywood Reporter in 2017 and 2018. In 2018, Time magazine named him one of the hundred most influential people in the world.
